Welcome to SecretLeague

We are a UK Dota 2 community for both new players and veterans alike. Whether you're looking for new teammates to party queue with, coaching, to get involved in our weekly inhouse matches, or to find new casting opportunities — you will be welcomed here!

Starting in August, we'll begin hosting our annual tournament, SecretLeague, open to all players regardless of rank!

Join us as we keep growing a fun, friendly community and celebrate our shared passion for Dota.

The Tournament

Format: Group stage into knockout rounds. Teams earn points in groups and advance to the bracket.

Registration: Enter as a solo player or register a full team. Save your draft locally and submit when ready.

Prizes: Community-funded prizes and eternal bragging rights. Details announced in Discord.

How It Works: 1. Register · 2. Draft using BortyGPT · 3. Play.
