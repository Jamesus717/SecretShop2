SecretLeague — Rules & Information
Team Eligibility

Each team must have at least one UK-based player on their roster. Teams are responsible for ensuring all roster information is accurate.

Smurfing Policy

Smurfing is not permitted. If you have previously used or currently own a smurf account, please inform tournament staff before participating. Failure to disclose alternate accounts may result in penalties or disqualification.

Advertising & Solicitation

Requesting money, promoting services, events, or communities within the server or through direct messages to participants is strictly prohibited without prior approval from staff.

Conduct

Treat all players, staff members, and community members with respect. Harassment, toxicity, discrimination, or unsportsmanlike behaviour will not be tolerated.

Staff Decisions

Tournament staff reserve the right to make rulings on situations not explicitly covered in these rules. All staff decisions are final.

Match Attendance & Late Arrivals

Teams are expected to be ready at the scheduled match time. Players or teams arriving late may receive penalties, including draft penalties, game forfeits, or match losses, depending on the delay and circumstances. If you anticipate being delayed, notify tournament staff and the opposing team's captain as soon as possible.

Stand-Ins

If you require a stand-in player, tournament staff must be notified at least two hours before the scheduled match start time. The stand-in player's Dotabuff profile must be provided to both tournament staff and the opposing team captain for approval. The player cannot be more than 500 MMR higher than the person they're replacing; they can be any rank below. Stand-ins may only participate once approval has been granted.

Contacting Staff

If any issues arise before, during, or after a match, contact tournament staff immediately. Staff are there to help resolve disputes, technical issues, and scheduling concerns.

About SecretLeague

SecretLeague is an amateur tournament that will be hosted yearly through our website and Discord server. We will live stream it on Twitch and YouTube.

Dates

📝 Sign-ups close — 3rd August

⚖️ Staff balancing & Division placement — 4th – 9th August

🏆 Group Stages begin — 10th August

🎯 Playoffs — start after group stages, depending on how many teams registered to play.

Format

The SecretLeague staff will balance the teams as best we can with two divisions — one lower and one upper. Group stages will be played as a BO2 for seeding. Once complete, teams will be placed into the winners or losers side of playoffs depending on group stage performance. The main event will follow with BO3 matches. Once the last two teams remain in each division, the Grand Finals will be a BO5 to determine the SecretLeague Champion!

When does SecretLeague begin?

SecretLeague is a yearly event with Group Stages starting from the 10th August.

How do I join SecretLeague?

You can register on the website through "Registration" at the top of the page.

Can I join regardless of rank?

Anyone can join, but since this is an amateur tournament staff are allowed to turn away teams that may be difficult to balance, so that everyone has a fair shot at 1st place.

Can I register as a solo player?

If you register solo a message will be sent into our "Looking for team" chat in the Discord server to help you find teammates. Solo Entry does not mean you will play in the tournament, it just makes you visible as available to other teams looking for players!

How do I get involved with casting and observing?

We are always looking for new casters and observers. Join the Discord and message one of the Admins if you'd like to try it out.

Where will SecretLeague be livestreamed?

Games will be streamed on our Twitch and YouTube channels.
